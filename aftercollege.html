College Finance:

Renting/Buying Houses

•Your rent/mortgage should never be more than 25% of your monthly income

Car Buying

•Cars begin to lose value the moment you drive them off the lot. To avoid losing massive amounts of money buy a lightly used car in cash.

•Used cars won't lose value as quickly as new cars and are often much cheaper.

Retirement

•15% of your monthly income should be put into a retirement fund. This will make sure that you have enough money for your retirement.

Emergency Fund

•You should have a account with your bank that has 3-6 months worth of monthly expenses in case of an emergency.

•If you have a commission based job like a real estate miracle then you should have closer to 6 months of expenses for financial security.
